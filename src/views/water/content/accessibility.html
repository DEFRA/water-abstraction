Service accessibility statement

We aim to make the Manage your water abstraction or impoundment licence service as accessible and easy to use as possible. All of our web pages are designed and tested to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines to level AA, and all except one successfully meet that standard.

Pages that do not meet the WCAG AA standard

Our feedback survey does not currently meet the same accessibility standards as the rest of our service. We are working to improve it.

How to contact us

If you want to give feedback about this service or have any questions about accessibility, please email us at water_abstractiondigital@environment-agency.gov.uk. We will respond within 5 working days.
